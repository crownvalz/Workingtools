Senior HR Consultant

Posted on May 29, 2025 · Full Time · Dar es Salaam

Job Description

The Senior HR Consultant will lead initiatives in employee engagement, talent acquisition, policy development, and HR systems improvement across the organization. The role is ideal for someone passionate about impact-driven work and organizational growth.

Employer

Heifer International is a reputable international NGO committed to eradicating poverty and hunger through sustainable agriculture, community empowerment, and capacity building.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Professional HR certification (e.g., SHRM, CIPD) is an added advantage.

Experience

  • Minimum of 5 years of progressive HR experience.
  • Experience working with international NGOs is highly desirable.
